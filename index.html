Why Learn Hindi?

Hindi is one of the world's most popular languages. It is spoken by around 260 million people across the globe as a native tongue, with another estimated 120 million people using Hindi as a second language in countries such as India and Nepal. Due to its vast number of speakers, Hindi is considered to be the fourth most-spoken language in the world, behind languages such as Chinese, Spanish, and English.

Learning Hindi will open up a whole new world of possibilities for you.
But what are these possibilities exactly?
